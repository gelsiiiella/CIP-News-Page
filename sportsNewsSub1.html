Belen, Solomon take charge as National U clinches UAAP Season 86 women’s volleyball title

By: Rom Anzures | May 15, 2024 | 07:58pm

NU’s Bella Belen. Photo from UAAP Media.

MANILA - National University are UAAP women’s volleyball champions yet again.

The Lady Bulldogs clinched their fourth league title -- and second in three seasons -- after they defeated University of Santo in a four-set thriller, 25-23, 23-25, 27-25, 25-16, during their Season 86 Finals Game 2 faceoff on Wednesday at the Mall of Asia Arena in Pasay City.

After both teams each earned wins in the first two sets, the Norman Miguel-coached team figured in a close encounter in the third after Xyza Gula, Jonna Perdido, Alyssa Solomon, Bella Belen, and Vange Alinsug exchanged hits in the frame.

NU then saw themselves down by a point, 25-24, but the freshly crowned Season 86 MVP took charge as she brought them to set point, 26-25, after an off-the-block kill.

In the next play, Sheena Toring finally dashed the third set after she scored through a block on Gula.

The Lady Bulldogs leaned on this momentum in the fourth and were able to build a 17-12 lead all thanks to the efforts of Belen as well as the costly errors of the Golden Tigresses, and this advantage proved to be enough as they finally reclaimed the championship after failing to defend it last season.

This is also the first time ever that National U won both men’s and women’s volleyball titles in the same season, and they are the first UAAP squad to do the feat since Ateneo de Manila University in Season 77.
